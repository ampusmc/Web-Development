Alexander M. Pujols

Alex was born on October 28th, 1993 in Manchester, NH. He was raised by his mother and has one younger siblng
After graduating from Manchester Central High School in 2012,he emediately enlisted into the United States Marine
Corps. After four months of training, he was assigned a permanent duty station at Mirimar Airbase, San Diego, California.
There he performed regular maintence on the CH-53E Helicopter. Alexander specialed as an avionics technician for
nearly four years before he sustained a serious injury on may 15th, 2015 while in the line of duty, ultimately leading to
the anatomical loss of his right eye. Alexander was then deemed unfit to serve and was honorably discharged in 2016.
On July 25, 2015 Alexander married his wife Rosa. Presently Alexander is residing in his home state of New Hampshire
with wis wife. He is also currently attending New Hampshire Technical Institute in his second year, pursuing an associates
degree in software development. His general inerest include electronics, cars, aviation, politics, space,sports and investing.
